About ROSTRUM

Data about current apps is extracted from ROSA and loaded into a spreadsheet for cleanup and attribute wrangling. The spread makes visualizing the data -- before and after -- difficult. This application provides views into that data including: Overview of all applications, Details of a specific Applicaiton, and details of attributes for all apps by category -- Administrative, Compliance, Description, Technical.

The data maintained by ROSA, and hence ROSTRUM, is deemed sensitive so the app must be located in contractor or government servers.

What's Suboptimal

  • Details and Technical pages have "Interface *" columns that are too wide for apps which list these without spaces: the HTML engine can't split the text into multiple lines to fit a cell.
  • After downloading, you're simply left where you invoked the download -- there's no confirmation page or redirect.
